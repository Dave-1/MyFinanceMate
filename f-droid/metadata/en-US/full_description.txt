DeepMoneyTracker is a privacy-focused, open-source expense tracker for Android that automatically tracks your income and expenses by parsing SMS messages from your bank.

Features:
• Automatic SMS transaction tracking from bank messages
• Smart auto-categorization with customizable rules
• Bill and payment reminder system with recurring schedules
• Detailed dashboard with income/expense breakdown
• Category-wise expense analysis with visual charts
• XML backup and restore functionality
• Biometric authentication for app security
• Material Design 3 with Material You theming
• Fully offline - no cloud dependencies
• No Google Play Services required

Supported transaction types:
• Credit/Debit card transactions
• UPI payments
• NEFT/RTGS/IMPS transfers
• Salary credits
• Bill payments

The app runs entirely on your device. Your financial data never leaves your phone.
